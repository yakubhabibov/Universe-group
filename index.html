UK



Studying at the University of England is one of the most affordable and prestigious ways to get higher education. England is one of the leading countries in the world in starting and doing business. And UK university graduates make up a good half of the global business elite.
14 UK universities are among the top 100 educational institutions in the world in terms of demand among employers


Undergraduate:
Duration of study - 3 years
Master's degree:
Duration of training - 1 year

Foreign students from the 1st year can work up to 20 hours a week and full time during the holidays.
Another advantage of studying in the UK is the opportunity to train in leading companies in the world. Many academic programs have paid internships of up to a year in their schedule! This not only supports financially, but also helps with further employment in England.
The prestige of British education and its standards are so high that you can look for work in any other country in the world, not limited to Europe. Moreover, it is much easier for students of British universities to obtain an American visa and participate in the Work&Travel summer program.
